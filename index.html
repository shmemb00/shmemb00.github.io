You've been phished!

If this was a real phishing campaign, many things could have been done to steal your information.

Here are some of the potential risks:

  • Identity theft: Attackers could have gathered personal details to impersonate you.
  • Account takeover: Your accounts could have been accessed or locked by malicious actors.
  • Bank fraud: Sensitive financial information could have been used to drain your bank account.
  • Malware installation: Harmful software could have been secretly installed on your device.
  • Data breach: Attackers could have gained access to your private and confidential files.
  • Ransomware: Your files could have been locked until a ransom is paid.
